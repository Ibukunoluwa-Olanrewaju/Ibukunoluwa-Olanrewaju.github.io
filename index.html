About Me

I'm a Healthcare Data Analyst passionate about using data to improve patient care, operational efficiency, and decision-making across healthcare organizations. With a background in Medical Laboratory Science, I combine clinical knowledge with analytical thinking to uncover insights that drive meaningful change.

My work spans healthcare analytics, business intelligence, and interactive dashboard development using SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau. I enjoy transforming complex data into clear, actionable insights that help organizations understand what's happening, why it's happening, and what to do next.

I'm currently expanding my expertise into Data Science and Machine Learning, with a focus on building predictive models that support better healthcare outcomes and smarter operational decisions.

Hospital Performance Analytics
